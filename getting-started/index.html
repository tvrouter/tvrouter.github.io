Getting Started
  1. Open your package;
  2. Plug the USB Dongle in the USB port of the NEXX box;
  3. Plug the micro USB power cable in the micro port and conect it to Power source.
  4. Find your TV Outlet and connect TV antenna cable to the USB dongle.
  5. Wait 30 seconds after powering up.
  6. Search for WIFI network TVRouter and connect.
